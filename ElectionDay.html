    • As the Presidential Election approaches, it is important to get young adults to vote. This will be a website for young adults from ages 18-29, however, is it also age appropriate for all who are eligible to vote in the New York State.

    This years candidates are Hillary Clinton, representing the Democratic Party, and Donald Trump, representing the Republican Party. The Presidential election is the most important election, which is held every 4 years. This election, in particular, is interesting because of the events that have occurred in the past 4 years that are being addressed by each candidate. Racial issues, health care, police brutality, violence, taxes, money, and education are the biggest factors during this election and it hits close to home for young adults which is why it is important to get the youth involved in voting.
